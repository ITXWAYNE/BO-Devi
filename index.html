    Taluso ya Puo


    Puo ki mukwa wakuambola kapa wa kufa maikuto ili milumo kapa lisupo ze yemela lika, likezo hamoho ni mihupulo. Puo ki mukwa wakuitusisa lilama zeitusiswa kwa kuambola (A language is a system of arbitrary vocal symbols used for human communication). Simuluho kapa litaba zapuo ze zwelela kwa lilimo ze sikiti za kwa mulaho. Puo, neli nto yenebulelwa feela nekusina kuñola. Kono zwelopili hainze ikena ni mufuta wa Puo, oitusisa kuñola ni ona wafita kapa kukena mwa Puo. Puo ki sinotolo ( key) kwa bupilo bwa butu.Puo ikona kutisa kapa kufelisa misunga kakuli ki sisebeliso seo siitiusisa kushimbulula bulumiwa, kuzwa kuya nani bona kuisa kuya teeleza. Sapir ( 1921), “ language is the necessary means of communication. It is the only indispensable instrument for creating the ties of the moment without which unified social action is impossible.” Puo ibulelwa kuba pilu (simbule) sa batu sakata, mi itisa tamahano mwahala batu sakata.


    Butokwa bwa Puo

    1. Ki mukwa wa kushimbulula mihupulo kuizusa kuyabulela kuiisa kwa batelezi.Ki ka kolo yakubulela ili lukona kuikabela maikuto ni batu babanwi. Ze likona kusingetetwa ka lisupo ni liponiso.
    2. Puo ibonisa maikuto aba babulela kapa batelezi. Tabo, sabo, bunyemi, kukomoka kaufela za bonahala mwa puo. Mwa ikutwela mutu, abulela, ze libonahala mwa puo yeitisiswa.
    3. Tamahano ni sibaka selupila hamoho cwaloni sibaka selupila kusona. Puo ki lukanda,itama babuleli ba yona.Batu ba babulela mushobo ulimuñwi batamahana hamoho ka puo. Kamutala,Silozi sitamahanya batu ba basibulela, Sishiwambo, sitamahanya babuleli basona, Otjiherero, sitamahanya b aba siambola, Si Khoesan ni sona si tamahanya ba basiambola. Sina Jesperson(1946), “ language is essential in establishing and maintaining relationship with other people. It is language which makes conversation possible between any two individuals. A purely human and non- instinctive method of communicating ideas, and hence a common languagebinds those who use it.Puo, itisa kuli batu batamahane.
    4. Ki mukwa o,batu bakona kubulela kapa kuambola ka linako zeba sikapila kuzona,kapa mane kubulela lika zebasika bona ka meto a bona. Batu bakona kubulela lika zeezahalile kapa zesataha. Kolo ye ibizwa displacement, ka Sikuwa. Batu bakona kubulela kuambola fa lika zesataha.Mi batu bakona kubuisana kuamana lika zesataha kapa mane ze kaezahala. 5.
    5. Puo niyona ina ni mata, akuputulula hande lika ka bukwala bwa ponahazo yetibile, expressive function. Ka litoko yafa toko,hatalimi feela bulumiwa bwa fa, kono ubonisa maikuto ahae,kutiba kwa hae, ni mubonelo wa hae wa lika za bulela.Ufa butungi bwa mubonelo wa hae wa sika.Mwa ku lapela, kuitilelela fa pila Mulimu, balapeli ba itusisa kolo ye ya puo(expressive function)
    6. Puo, ikauhanya mutu kwa lifolofolo.Folofolo aina maemo a Puo, ao mutu una ni ona.Ki mutu, feela yana ni puo, yeutwahala, ya ambola kwa batu babañwi. Mutu uambola lipuo za lifasi: Silozi, Sisubiya, Sikuwa etc. Cwale ki mañi ya kileabona folofolo, yeambola Silozi, Sisubiya kapa Sikuwa? Ki mutu feela yana ni lilama za puo, zesebeza kumutusa kuli a ambole.
    7. Puo iyemela sizo, ki ka puo ili mo sicaba sishimbulula lika za sizo ze swana sina bulapeli,bumulimu, mupilelo, misumo ya bupilo, sizo. Sizo si tamahani ni puo. Mi Puo, ishimbile sizo. Halukoni kutalima za Puo, lusa putululi sizo ni kubona fo sitomile.

    Orthography of Silozi


    Silozi Orthography 3 (muñolelo wabulalu)


    Silozi orthography yabulalu ilusupeza mukwa kamo milumo yalulimi lwasilozi iñolelwa, Silozi isebelisa alfabhete yasikuwa sakale (latin-based) kwakuyemela milumo yenwi yefumaneha mwamushobo. Kikutalusa feela kuli muñolelo 3 kikuñola kakukopanya mazwi. Kmt haasikaluutwa.


    Key Features of Silozi Orthography:


    Mubizezo: kimukwa kapa nzila moo linzwi libizezwa kapa puo ibulelelwa.

    Alfabethe: Silozi isebelisa alfabhete ya sikuwa, kono ikopanyize nilitaku zenwi zesiyo mwasikuwa, ili zetusa kueza milumo yefumaneha feela mwaSilozi.

    Matumanosi(Vowels): a, e, I, o, u

    Matumamoho(Consonants): b, c, ch, d, f, g, h, j, k, l, m, n, ñ, p, s, sh, t, w, y, z

    Special Characters: The letter “ñ” represents a nasal sound similar to the “ny” in “canyon.”

    Tone: Silozi is a tonal language, meaning that the pitch or tone used when pronouncing a word can change its meaning. Tones are typically marked as high or low. Mutala 1.

    2. 2. FERDINAND DE SAUSSURE SEMIOTIC THEORY Kunyakisisa kwa lipuo ka kuitusisa lisupo (Semiotic) Puo, ki lisupo. Fapuokukopanezi lisupo. Manzwi ni lipulelo kaufela anani fa supa.     3. 3. Kutalusa Musebezi waManzwi mwaLipulelo (mifuta yamazwi) (parts of speech) Mwatuto yaluna yamusebezi walikalulo zamanzwi mwalipulelo lukakala kutalusa kuli pulelo kiñi. Pulelo ki sikwata samanzwi sesilufa muhupulo otezi ili ofela ka kuto, makalo kapa liswayo lapuzo mi sina ni likalulo zepeli. Likalulo ze, ki toho yapulelo ni kalulo yeñwi yabubeli yeo ina ni liezi ni liñusa lelilutaluseza kaza toho yapulelo yeo. Mwatuto mo lukanyakisisa kaza misebezi yamanzwi ashutanashutana mwalipulelo. Haluna feela kunyakisisa kaza misebezi yamanzwi mwalipulelo kono lukaambola hape kazeama likalulo zamanzwi ona atwi mwaSikuwa kuli ki ‘Parts of Speech’ kapa ‘word classes’. Likalulo zamanzwi lilusupeza kuli musebezi wazona ki ufi mwalipulelo zelili kuzona. Kabukuswani, lukali manzwi a, abapala kalulo mañi mwalipulelo. Kamutala lukona kubulela kuli, manzwi alatelela kaufela ki mabizo; mukwakwa, Monde, ndu, mota, mutu ncc. Hakuna taba kuli ki mabizotota kapa ki mabizo feela kaufelaa ona awela mwakalulo ya mabizo. MwaSikuwa luna ni likalulo zamanzwi zelobabeli kono mwaSilozi mona, kuna ni likalulo zeñatanyana. Lukaambola kuzeñwi zalikalulo zeo kono zeñwi haluna kuliambola bakeñisa kuli haki zabutokwa kanako yacwale. Lutaalula hape likalulo zamanzwi ze mwalikalulo zepeli ili zeli; kalulo yapili kiyeo yena ni likalulo zamanzwi afumaneha mwalipuo kaufela (universal parts of speech) mi yabubeli ki kalulo yeo yesafumanehi mwalipuo kaufela (non-universal parts of speech). Kakufa mutala lubone pili kuli ki likalulo mañi zamanzwi zefumaneha mwaSikuwa. Haluituti Sikuwa kono kubutokwa kuli mubone siswaniso sa zelubulela kakuitusisa puo yeñozi lika zeñata fataba ye.       e) Masupi: Masupi ki manzwi asupa lika zeli bukaufi ni mubuleli (sibuabui), bukaufi ni yabulelelwa kapa zeli kusibuabui ni yabulelelwa sina;
